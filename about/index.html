Welcome!

This is the personal website of xrdrsp. Only posts (in the form of an article, see also Archives) are included in the website at present. The frequency of updating is unpredictable. The posts are non-technical. In the future, more topics or forms may be involved. Some recordings of my piano performance which I think is worth making public may in the future be uploaded here, while some details are still under consideration. (see also here).

Due to my poor English level, some of the posts may be written in Simplified Chinese or in both languages, Simplified Chinese and English. Occasionally, correctness in posts in English couldn't be maintained, nor can clarity sometimes. Apart from these, there may exist sorts of other inaccuracies on this website, and thus, please always feel free to report them. I also recommend that you not forget to AT xrdrsp when opening issues, pull requests, or discussions if possible in order to get my attention.

As for feedbacks under some posts with an explicit theme, my only request is that the discussions keep creative, constructive, polite, at least tangentially relevant to the topic at hand; those which are spam, off-topic, self-promoting, unfriendly, racist, extremist, pornographic, illegal, or otherwise not fulfilling the above requests shall summarily be deleted, as appropriate. Hence, the comment rating system is enabled when using Disqus, yet those ratings do not unconditionally represent the quality of the feedback. Please appropriately discuss and leave suggestions and comments.

Previously, some other blog addresses were being used. However, they are no longer in use at present and all of the posts in previous blogs should be considered expired unless stating additionally. For those which are migrated to this website, ones on this blog should prevail.

Technical Remarks

This blog is currently using the Untitled Theme by myself. You are welcome to copy the theme. Moreover, I would appreciate it if a reference or citation to the origin .css file is provided.

Computer Modern and PingFang SC are used as the typefaces. See also theme.css.

Disqus is used as the comment system. In most cases, Disqus is displayed under the pages, but meanwhile, possibly, it is disabled to be observed in some specific areas, according to specific laws and policies.

To show $\LaTeX$ equations, I embedded MathJax. See also head.html.

The formatting of the date & time is DD-MM-YYYY HH:MM:SS. To take one example, 20-11-2006 11:40:00 means November 20th, 11:40:00 in year 2006.
