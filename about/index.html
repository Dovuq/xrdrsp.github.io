Technical Remarks

This blog is currently using the Untitled Theme by Dovuq and me.

Computer Modern and Noto Sans are used as main typefaces. See also theme.css.

To show mathematics notation with $\LaTeX$, I embedded MathJax 2.7.9. See also head.html.

The formatting of the date & time is YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS. To take one example, 2006-11-20 11:40:00 represents forty past eleven, November 20th in year 2006.
