About

Welcome!

This is the personal website of xrdrsp. Posts (in the form of an article, see also Archives) and audios are included in the website at present. The frequency of updating is unpredictable. The posts are non-technical. In the future, more topics or forms may be involved. Some more recordings of my piano performance which I think is worth making public may in the future be uploaded here, while some details are still under consideration.

Due to my English level, some of the posts may be written in Simplified Chinese or in both languages, Simplified Chinese and English. Occasionally, correctness in posts in English couldn't be maintained, nor can clarity sometimes. Apart from these, there may exist sorts of other inaccuracies on this website, and please always feel free to report them. I also recommend that you AT xrdrsp when opening issues, pull requests, or discussions if possible in order to get my attention.

There are two ways you can leave feedbacks: via the Discussion Board using Cusdis, and via Disqus under most pages. In the Discussion Board, the topics of comments are not circumscribed. As for feedbacks with the commenting system Disqus under some posts with an explicit theme, my only request is that the discussions keep constructive, polite, at least tangentially relevant to the topic at hand; those which are spam, off-topic, self-promoting, unfriendly, racist, extremist, pornographic, illegal, or otherwise not fulfilling the above requests shall summarily be deleted, as appropriate. Hence, the comment rating system is enabled when using Disqus under posts.

Previously, some other blog addresses were being used. However, they are no longer in use at present and all of the posts in previous blogs should be considered expired unless stating additionally. For those which are migrated to this website, ones on this blog should prevail. I also used some blogging systems, such as Hexo, and Wordpress.

Technical Remarks

This blog is currently using the Untitled Theme by Dovuq and me.

Computer Modern and PingFang SC are used as the main typefaces. See also theme.css.

Disqus and Cusdis are used as the commenting systems. In most cases, Disqus is displayed under the pages, but meanwhile, possibly, it is disabled to be observed in some specific areas, according to specific laws and policies. Cusdis is only displayed in the Discussion Board.

To show $\LaTeX$ equations, I embedded MathJax. See also head.html.

The formatting of the date & time is DD-MM-YYYY HH:MM:SS. To take one example, 20-11-2006 11:40:00 means November 20th, 11:40:00 in year 2006.
