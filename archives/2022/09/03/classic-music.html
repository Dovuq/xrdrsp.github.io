A Report of Works of Classic Music I Have Been Listening To This Summer

03-09-2021

This summer, I have been discovering and listening to more classic music works, mainly composed by Pretty Damned Quick Bach (Peter Schickele), Ludwig van Beethoven, Louis-Hector Berlioz, Johannes Brahms, Josef Anton Bruckner, Frédéric François Chopin, Achille-Claude Debussy, Dovuq, Franz Joseph Haydn, Gustav Mahler, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Sergei Sergeyevich Prokofiev, Nikolai Andreyevitch Rimsky-Korsakov, Arnold Schoenberg (Schönberg), Alexander Nikolayevich Scriabin, Dmitri Dmitriyevich Shostakovich, Franz Schubert, Igor Fyodorovich Stavinsky, Leyou Wang, Xilin Wang and Xrdrsp.

Below would be a list of some works that I feel like listening to this summer (i. e. those I only listened to before this summer, but not in this summer are not included; those I listened both before and in this summer can be included), and is separated into two parts. Part I consists of music works that made me (nearly) break out crying (i. e. they attract me so much!); Part II contains other works that I think is worth of having it on this website. Some comments on works may also be left below.
